Background & History 背景と歴史

Originating in 1999 in the artificial island of Odaiba, Tokyo, the Odaiba Institution of Science and Technology (OST, お台場科学技術大学) has been at service for the past 25 years to provide funding to local and international innovations. We allow the ease of access to science and technology, so even you can innovate. Let us create a better future together, for society as a whole.

The Odaiba Institution of Science and Technology is merely a fictitious organization and does not exist in the real world. The OST is heavily inspired by Miraikan, better known as the National Museum of Emerging Science and Innovation (日本科学未来館), located on the artificial island of Odaiba, Tokyo.
