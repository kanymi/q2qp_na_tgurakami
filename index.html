Background & History 背景と歴史

Originating in 1999 in the artificial island of Odaiba, Tokyo, the Odaiba Institution of Science and Technology (OST, お台場科学技術大学) has been at service for the past 25 years to provide funding to local and international innovations. We try fixing environmental, astronomical, geological, biological, biochemical, medical, and so much more other problems pertaining to Science and Technology. As of 2024, over 900 innovators and researchers have joined us in providing a safer space in the world. Join us at the Odaiba Institute of Science and Technology, as not only we sustain Japan, we sustain mankind.
