Who are we? 私たちは誰ですか？

We are the Odaiba Institution of Science and Technology (OST, お台場科学技術大学). As a collective, we support many innovators all around the world in their own individual projects. In this new generation of technology, humanity needs to keep up. That is why we at OST are here, prepared to advance society in the fields of STEM at any circumstance. From Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics, we are up for it. We are OST.

Vision and Mission ビジョンとミッション

Vision

We create the pavement for innovators all around the globe to succeed in their projects, based in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics. We are the speaker from thousands of individuals around the globe who want their research shown to the world, making an impact.

Mission

Our goal is to advance the field of Science and Technology as far as possible. From environmental, astronomical, technological, and medical issues, we will solve all.

How to Help? 助ける方法？

Donate:

Help us in donating to OST, by funding individuals in their projects. Click the "Donate" button on the top right to learn more.

Innovate:

Innovate and work on your own projects, and maybe you will be the one to successfully pioneer in the fields of STEM.

The Odaiba Institution of Science and Technology is merely a fictitious organization and does not exist in the real world. The OST is heavily inspired by Miraikan, better known as the National Museum of Emerging Science and Innovation (日本科学未来館), located on the artificial island of Odaiba, Tokyo.
